sorry javascript is required to play space-keys 
                You're objective is to fly your space craft around the moon and land successfully! 
        
                Use the commands:

                -fire(# of seconds);
                -sleep(# of seconds);
                -turn_left(# of seconds);
                -turn_right(# of seconds);

                For example to take off, turn around and land again:

                fire(1);
                turn_left(3);
                fire(2);
                turn_right(3);
                fire(1);

                Important: Semicolons are required after each commands.
