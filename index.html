                You're objective is to fly your space craft around the moon and land successfully! 
        
                Use the commands:

                -fire(# of seconds);
                -sleep(# of seconds);
                -turn(number of degrees);
          			- positive 180 will turn around clockwise to the right
          			- negative 360 will turn 1 full circle to the left

                For example to take off, power left, power right, and land:

			fire(4);
			turn(-90);
			fire(1);
			turn(180);
			fire(1);
			turn(-90);
			fire(3);

                Important: Semicolons are required after each commands.
                
                To land, you rocket must be upright (0 degrees) and traveling at less then 8 m/s
                
                Watch out for gravity and have fun!
